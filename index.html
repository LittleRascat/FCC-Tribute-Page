Important events in Sir Isaac Newton's life.

  • 1642 ~ Born in Woolsthorpe-by-Colsterworth, England
  • 1661 ~ Was admitted to Trinity College, Cambridge
  • 1665 ~ Begins developing what becomes Calculus
  • 1668 ~ Earns his M.A. from Trinity College
  • 1687 ~ Published Principia where he states the three laws of motion and defines the law of universal gravitation
  • 1703 ~ Becomes president of the Royal Society
  • 1704 ~ Published Opticks where he presents his theory of light
  • 1705 ~ Was knighted by Queen Anne
  • 1727 ~ Died in London and was buried in Westminster Abbey
